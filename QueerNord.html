Photography Project

What is that?

Since joining Queer Nord in January 2023, I have been eager to take on responsibilities similar to those I held at Aalborg Pride. One of these responsibilities is a photography project that I am currently developing. While it is still in its early stages, I am excited to share more information as it becomes available. Highlights

Tiderne skifter

I was the production leader for a production called "Tiderne Skifter," which was my longest and most professional project to date. From the conception of the idea to the premiere at our school, I played a critical role in every stage of the production process. As the production leader, I was responsible for planning the entire production process, and ensuring the final product met our high standards of quality.

SoMe Creator at Aalborg Pride

From 2020 to 2022, I was responsible for Aalborg Pride's social media presence, particularly on Instagram. I developed and executed a content strategy that centered partly around interviews with queer individuals. The theme of these interviews was "We are not alone," which aimed to highlight the experiences of LGBTQ+ individuals living in smaller cities, where it can often feel isolating.

Aalborg Pride Afterparty

In 2022, I successfully planned and executed the official Afterparty at Aalborg Pride. This involved a series of meetings with the venue and volunteers, briefing everybody and making sure our expectations aligned. The event was attended by 500 guests, and I received only positive feedback, indicating that it was a successful first-time event.
