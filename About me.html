Who am I?

Who is Emma?

The basics

Let´s start, my name is Emma Seddiki, and I am a 23-year-old queer creator from Denmark.

My passion for media began when I attended Frederiksberg Filmværksted at the age of approximately 12. It was there that I discovered my love for film and the way it impacts its audience. While initially drawn to being in front of the camera, I soon realized the immense value and impact of planning and production. This realization led me to attend Produktionshøjskolen I Brøndby, where I gained experience as an official production leader.

I am currently pursuing a degree in Communication and Digital Media, which has been instrumental in my understanding of the impact that media has on society. My coursework has exposed me to various aspects of media, including communication through media, website creation and the impact social media has on us. This education has been invaluable in shaping my skills as a creative media professional.

addition to my academic pursuits, I have been fortunate enough to volunteer my skills as a SoMe creator for Aalborg Pride. This experience has been a meaningful way for me to contribute to an important cause, while also honing my creative abilities. Through this work, I have gained a deeper understanding of the power of social media and its ability to amplify voices and create positive change. Overall, my passion for media has been a driving force in both my academic and professional endeavors. I am excited to continue exploring this dynamic field and using my skills to make a positive impact in the world.

Highlights

Tiderne skifter

I was the production leader for a production called "Tiderne Skifter," which was my longest and most professional project to date. From the conception of the idea to the premiere at our school, I played a critical role in every stage of the production process. As the production leader, I was responsible for planning the entire production process, and ensuring the final product met our high standards of quality.

SoMe Creator at Aalborg Pride

From 2020 to 2022, I was responsible for Aalborg Pride's social media presence, particularly on Instagram. I developed and executed a content strategy that centered partly around interviews with queer individuals. The theme of these interviews was "We are not alone," which aimed to highlight the experiences of LGBTQ+ individuals living in smaller cities, where it can often feel isolating.

Aalborg Pride Afterparty

In 2022, I successfully planned and executed the official Afterparty at Aalborg Pride. This involved a series of meetings with the venue and volunteers, briefing everybody and making sure our expectations aligned. The event was attended by 500 guests, and I received only positive feedback, indicating that it was a successful first-time event.
