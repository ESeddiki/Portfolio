Info

Welcome to my portfolio website. Allow me to introduce myself. My name is Emma Seddiki, and my pronouns are they/them .

Currently, I am pursuing a degree in Communication and Digital Media with specialization in Interactive Digital Media at Aalborg University, where i graduated in June 2024.

My passion for media, particularly film, has been a constant in my life. I began my journey in the world of film at the age of 11, participating in a Film Workshop that continued to inspire me until I turned 16. As I grew older, my interests expanded to encompass various forms of media, leading me to gain experience in SoMe management, photography and film production.

Tiderne skifter

As the production leader for "Tiderne Skifter" (Times Change), I held an important role in shaping every aspect of the production, from concept to premiere. Beyond production leadership, I also managed casting and costumes showcasing my versatility. This experience allowed me to develop strong project management skills, teamwork, and attention to detail. It provided valuable insights into creative project management, improving my adaptability and leadership in a collaborative environment.

Aalborg Pride Afterparty

In 2022, as an active participant in Aalborg Pride, I lead the successful planning and execution of the official Afterparty. My role encompassed a series of meticulous meetings with the venue and dedicated volunteers, where I effectively communicated our vision and ensured seamless coordination. The event garnered an impressive attendance of over 400 guests, and the overwhelmingly positive feedback underscored its resounding success.

Creator of Spring ud i det

"Spring ud i det" was conceived and developed during my studies in Communication and Digital Media. As part of a course on Transmedial Design and Communication, I created this pilot episode to explore queer identities and young adult challenges. The project integrates multiple media platforms, including a podcast and social media, to engage viewers and facilitate meaningful conversations. My personal experiences as a queer individual fueled my passion for authentic representation and storytelling in this series.
