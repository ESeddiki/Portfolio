What is my educational background?

Here is a brief overview over my prior and current education situation

Aalborg University

Studying Communication and Digital Media at Aalborg University. Getting my bachelors degree June 2024.

Roskilde Festival Højskole

Studied leadership at Roskilde Festival folk high school in 2020.

Københavns VUC

Studied at Copenhagen high school for adults and passed in 2018. Chose media as a selected course.

Produktionshøjskolen i Brøndby

Studied Film and media at Produktionshøjskolen i Brøndby in 2016. Here i learned about bigger cameras and how to operate them.
I also acted as production leader on our 25 min long shortfilm "Tiderne Skifter"

Frederiksberg Filmværksted

From 2012 to 2016 , I attended Frederiksberg Filmværksted, where I received training in creating short films and videos. My time at Frederiksberg Filmværksted was a formative experience, as it sparked my passion for the film and media industry. During my time there, I created a variety of short films, and my instructors regularly challenged me, and the groups I were in, with diverse film-making tasks and competitions.
