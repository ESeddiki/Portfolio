About

The concept

"Spring ud i det" is a transmedia project that primarily revolves around a pilot episode, but it extends its narrative and engagement through multiple media platforms. Inspired by the innovative storytelling approach of the Norwegian series "SKAM," "Spring ud i det" takes a similar immersive and interactive route. However, it focuses on a demographic that often lacks representation: young adults in their 20s and queer individuals.

Core Elements:

Pilot Episode: The heart of "Spring ud i det" lies in its pilot episode, which delves into the lives of four main characters navigating the complexities of queer identity and young adulthood.

Podcast: Complementing the pilot, the podcast facilitates deeper conversations about the themes explored, such as identity, relationships, and societal challenges. It provides a platform for extended discussion and personal reflections.

Instagram Profile: The Instagram profile offers behind-the-scenes content, character insights, and interactive elements that enhance viewer engagement. It allows the audience to connect more personally with the characters and their stories.

By adopting a transmedia approach, "Spring ud i det" not only tells compelling stories but also fosters a community around the series. This project aims to fill the gap in representation for young adults and queer individuals, offering authentic narratives and a space for meaningful dialogue.

Podcast

And mastering the good conversation

The "Spring ud i det" podcast is an essential part of the transmedia project, designed to facilitate in-depth conversations about queer identity and the challenges faced by young adults in their 20s. This podcast complements the pilot episode of the series, offering a deeper dive into the themes explored in the show.

Episode Highlights:

Hosted by Jamie and Thomas: The first episode features hosts Jamie and Thomas, who introduce themselves and the series. They discuss the inspiration behind "Spring ud i det" and what listeners can expect from both the podcast and the series. In this episode, Alex and Loui, two main characters from the series, join Jamie and Thomas to share their experiences. This segment provides listeners with personal insights and deeper connections to the characters from the show.

Discussion on Pronouns: A significant portion of the episode is dedicated to discussing the importance of pronouns in the queer community. Alex and Loui share their personal experiences and insights on how pronouns influence identity and everyday interactions.

Queer Experiences in Denmark: The hosts and guests delve into their personal journeys as queer individuals living in Denmark. They discuss societal expectations, the process of coming out, and the unique challenges they face. This segment aims to provide listeners with a relatable and authentic perspective on queer life.

Interactive Elements: The podcast encourages listener engagement through interactive elements such as Q&A sessions and discussions on social media platforms. This fosters a community around the series, allowing fans to share their own stories and experiences, and creating a dynamic and inclusive dialogue.

Educational and Supportive Role: Beyond storytelling, the podcast aims to educate and support its listeners. It addresses key issues such as mental health, relationships, and self-acceptance, providing practical advice and resources. By offering a platform for open and honest discussions, the podcast strives to empower queer individuals and promote a greater understanding and acceptance within the broader community.

Social media

And online personas

The social media presence of "Spring ud i det" was designed to be an immersive transmedia experience. Initially, the plan was for each of the four main characters to have their own TikTok and Instagram accounts, allowing for a deeper engagement with the audience. However, due to the constraints of producing the project alone, this was not fully implemented.

Instagram Profile:

The main Instagram profile for "Spring ud i det" currently features the series trailer and teasers, introducing the podcast and the upcoming series. Additionally, there is a presentation of Alex, one of the main characters. The intention was to also present the other characters in a similar manner, providing a comprehensive introduction to the cast.

Overview:

1. Trailers and Teasers: The profile includes the official trailer and teasers for the podcast and series, building anticipation among followers.

2. Character Introductions: So far, Alex has been introduced with detailed posts. The plan was to continue this with the other main characters, giving followers insight into their backgrounds and personalities.

Future Aspirations: While it was not feasible to create separate accounts for each character, the current Instagram profile effectively engages the audience and builds excitement for the series. Future expansions could include more character presentations and additional content to enhance the transmedia storytelling approach of "Spring ud i det."
