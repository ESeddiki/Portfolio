Info

Welcome to my portfolio website. Allow me to introduce myself. I am Emma Seddiki, and my pronouns are they/them .

Currently, I am pursuing a degree in Communication and Digital Media at Aalborg University, with an expected completion date in June 2024.

My passion for media, particularly film, has been a constant in my life. I began my journey in the world of film at the age of 11, participating in a Film Workshop that continued to inspire me until I turned 16. As I grew older, my interests expanded to encompass various forms of media, leading me to gain experience in social media management, photography, and, of course, film production.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to discover a collection of my work and projects that reflect my dedication to the realm of digital media.

Educational background

Aalborg University

Attending the Bachelor's program in Communication and Digital Media, at Aalborg University. Graduating June 2024.

Roskilde Festival Folk High School

Majored in Leadership, with elective courses in Green Entrepreneurship, Local Storytelling and Gender. I attended the school from January to June of 2020.

Higher Preparatory Examination

Studied at Copenhagen Higher Preparatory Examination and graduated in 2018. I chose Media studies as an elective subject and achieved the grade B.

Production High School

Followed the Film and Television Program at the Production High School in Brøndby in 2016. Here i learned about leadership, film equipment and production management.
I also acted as production leader on our shortfilm "Tiderne Skifter".

Highlights

Tiderne skifter

As the production leader for "Tiderne Skifter" (Times Change), I held an important role in shaping every aspect of the production, from concept to premiere. Beyond production leadership, I also managed casting and costumes showcasing my versatility. This experience allowed me to develop strong project management skills, teamwork, and attention to detail. It provided valuable insights into creative project management, improving my adaptability and leadership in a collaborative environment.

Aalborg Pride Afterparty

In 2022, as an active participant in Aalborg Pride, I lead the successful planning and execution of the official Afterparty. My role encompassed a series of meticulous meetings with the venue and dedicated volunteers, where I effectively communicated our vision and ensured seamless coordination. The event garnered an impressive attendance of over 400 guests, and the overwhelmingly positive feedback underscored its resounding success.

Board member at Queer Nord

I volunteer as a board member at Queer Nord, dedicated to promoting LGBTQ+ rights and diversity in Aalborg. My contributions include co-establishing a diversity center in Aalborg, active involvement in events like Aalborg Pride, facilitating debates, supporting local LGBTQ+ organizations in Northern Jutland, and advocating passionately for inclusivity and equality in our community.
